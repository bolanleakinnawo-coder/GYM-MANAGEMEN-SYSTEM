What We Offer

Everything You Need
Under One Roof

PeakFit is equipped with a full range of facilities and programs designed to support every aspect of your fitness — from strength and cardio to flexibility and recovery.

01

Strength & Weight Training

A fully equipped weights floor featuring free weights, resistance machines, and dedicated lifting platforms for all fitness levels.

02

Cardio & Endurance

Modern treadmills, rowing machines, cycling stations, and ellipticals to power your cardiovascular fitness goals.

03

Group Fitness Classes

High-energy group sessions including aerobics, Zumba, HIIT, and spin classes led by motivating certified instructors.
